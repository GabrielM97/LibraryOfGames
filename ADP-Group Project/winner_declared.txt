You freeze checking your ticket number again. How could this be? Sighs of
relief and moans of disappointment come from the crowd.
The voices soon die down and all eyes are on you and the paper in
your hands.

Suited Man:   And there I thought we would have to draw again... Making
              history, ladies and gentleman, we have our adventurer!

The suited man waves you forward and calls for cheers and whoops from the 
bystanders. 
The crowd backs away from you, like the sea from high land. Your hands shake
as you move forward toward the suited man and then into a waiting vehicle...
